Skin Types

Know Your Skin

Understanding your skin type is the first step to a great routine. Click any card to ask Skindle about it.

💧
Oily Skin

Prone to shine, enlarged pores, and breakouts. Needs balance and control without over-stripping.

🌵
Dry Skin

Tends to feel tight or flaky. Needs deep hydration and barrier-supporting ingredients.

☯️
Combination Skin

Oily in the T-zone, dry on the cheeks. Requires zone-specific care strategies.

🌸
Sensitive Skin

Easily irritated, prone to redness. Avoid harsh actives, fragrances, and high concentrations.
